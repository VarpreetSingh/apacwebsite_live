Team Leader for Counselling Team

Job Description

No. of Position: 1

  • Lead the team of Counsellors and responsible for achieving admission targets for PGDM through the team.
  • Monitor & Guide the calling activities of the counselling team.
  • Ensure productivity and effectiveness of the counselling team to generate admissions.
  • Train, orient and equip the counselling team for hand holding prospective students in the admission cycle.
  • Represent the institute in appropriate forums / events and lead from the front in inculcating a result oriented approach.
  • Preferably from Education industry.
  • Conversant with MS Excel, PowerPoint.
  • Excellent communication skills, with fluency in English.
  • Result Oriented & Good Interpersonal skills.
  • Team Player
