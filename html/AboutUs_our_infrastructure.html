Auditorium


Asia-Pacific Institute of Management has very huge Auditorium with the capacity of 200+ seats. The Auditorium has named after “Dr. B.B. Verma” one of The Board of Governors and Former Director, Bata India Ltd. This spacious Auditorium is fully Air condition with all state of Art Facilities. The foundation stone of the college auditorium was put up in 1996. We are proud of our college Auditorium. It can accommodate around 200+ people. Many events like Annual day, cultural festival, Mr. and Miss AIM Contest, Dramas, Departmental Fests, seminars etc. have been staged on it.



Cafeteria


AIM cafeteria located at Campus building rooftop. Cafeteria is fully air conditioned and having well sitting arrangements. Our Canteen serves a full menu of Breakfast and lunch options including hot food items. Cafeteria Timings are 9:00am to 5:30pm, Monday to Saturday available for All Students, Faculty and Staff Members.



IT Infrastructure


Asia-Pacific Institute of Management has truly world class, campus-wide state of the art information technology tools that are designed to meet the computing and communication needs of the institute, which has a fast, reliable and rugged computer network of more than 475 Nodes. The institute has more than 32 laptops issued to its faculty, staff and students. To provide printing and other facilities, the institute has 47 printers and scanners. In order to facilitate proper teaching aid, all classrooms here are equipped with projectors and audio systems. Hostels are also connected through Wi-Fi. All the floor are connected through layer 2 & 3 switches to provide 1Gbps connectivity at the user end. Wireless network with 21+ access points is also enabled in the Campus.

Internet Access: The internet connectivity is through 16 Mbps (1:1) Mbps fibre link through an Internet leased line from ISP. Internet connection range is provided through both LAN and Wi-Fi AIM Campus

Data Centre: AIM has a full-fledged data centre of its own. It hosts 5 Servers and network unified storage. The data centre is powered by redundant UPS backup to ensure maximum uptime. All servers are connected to Internet by public IPs

UTM Firewall: Our entire network has running under Cyberoam UTM.

IP Address IPv4: The institute has its own /29 public IPV4 address block.

Video Conferencing: Polycom VC System is available with optional ISDN line & also connected with Internet.

UPS: We have 2 x 40KVA (Configured in N+1 in redundant mode) Dedicated UPS powering for critical IT infrastructure.

CCTV Surveillance: All class rooms, computer labs, corridor are under CCTV surveillance
