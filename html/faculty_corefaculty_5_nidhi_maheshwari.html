INDIVISUAL PROFILE

Dr. Nidhi Maheshwari

Associate Professor

Area : HR and Organization Behavior

Experience: 13 years

MBA,LL.B. ,Ph.D

Contact Details

nidhi@asiapacific.edu
011-42094842



Dr. Nidhi Maheshwari has been in the research and teaching profession for over a decade. She has completed her Ph.D. and LL.B. degree from Mohan Lal Sukhadiya University, Rajasthan. She also holds a MBA degree from Banasthali Vidyapeeth. During her doctoral studies in the area of Emotional and Spiritual quotient, she had widely published papers in refereed international and national journals. Before joining Asia-Pacific Institute of Management, she was associated with JK Lakshmipath University, Rajasthan, as Assistant Professor. Her areas of research include Spirituality, Value system and Indian ethos. Recently, she was awarded with the “Best research paper” in the IIM- Shillong Sustainability conference (2015). Two Ph.D. scholars are pursuing research under her supervision.
