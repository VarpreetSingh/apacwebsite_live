Nusantra PGRI University

https://unpkediri.ac.id

Founded in 2006, Universitas Nusantara PGRI Kediri (Nusantara PGRI University of Kediri) is a private higher education institution located in the small city of Kediri, East Java. Officially accredited/recognized by the Kementerian Riset, Teknologi, dan Pendidikan Tinggi (Ministry of Research, Technology and Higher Education of the Republic of Indonesia), Universitas Nusantara PGRI Kediri (UNP Kediri) is a small coeducational higher education institution. Universitas Nusantara PGRI Kediri (UNP Kediri) offers courses and programs leading to officially recognized higher education degrees in several areas of study. UNP Kediri also provides several academic and non-academic facilities and services to students including a library, as well as administrative services.

Why study in Indonesia ?
Indonesia provides a vibrant, multi-cultural environment for international students. About 6,000 international students currently study in Indonesia, with most of them coming from Malaysia. The Management programs, especially those at private institutions, are taught in English or other languages for students not proficient in the Indonesian language. MBA programs taught in English are especially popular. If you’re interested in something different or looking to get ahead in the Asian century, Indonesian is one of your best options. or a beginner it is not unusual for students to reach a basic conversation level within 1-2 months of intensive study. Learning Indonesian will give you an eclectic mix of Eastern, Western and Islamic influences. The experience will challenge you to think outside of your comfort zone and engage with unfamiliar values and practices. It will also allow you to learn more about yourself and your own culture by forcing you to reflect on your cultural pre-conceptions – for example, on time, on religion, and on politics – and how they differ to those in Indonesia.




University of Liberia

http://www.ul.edu.lr

The University of Liberia is the Flagship University of the Nation with an enrollment of about 34,000 students, 450 full time faculty (500 part-time faculty) and 1,200 staff on five campuses, Old & New Fendall, Medical, Straz-Sinje, and Capitol Hill. Undergraduate colleges include Liberia College of Social Science and Humanities (5760 students), W.V.S. Tubman Teachers’ College (1896 students), the College of Business and Public Administration (12,435 students), the T.J.R. Faulkner College of Science and Technology (7,828 students), the William R. Tolbert College of Agriculture and Forestry (3,706 students), the College of General Studies (800 students – declared majors are combined in other colleges), and Straz-Sinje (100 students).

Who should opt ?
If you’re an adrenaline junkie, Africa is the continent for you and you’re looking for adventure, wildlife and unique travel opportunities, Africa can provide a home for you. Developing countries might not seem as comfortable or instantaneous as life in the United States, but the kindness of the locals is remarkable. The people will help you fulfill your dreams while allowing you to experience their world. If you want something totally different for your study abroad experience, consider Africa and even Botswana as your final destination.
