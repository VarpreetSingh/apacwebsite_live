Nai Roshni



About Nai Roshni
Empowerment of women per se is not only essential for equity, but also constitutes a critical element in our fight for poverty reduction, economic growth and strengthening of civil society. Women and children are always the worst sufferers in a poverty stricken family and need support. Empowering women, especially mothers, is even more important as it is in homes that she nourishes, nurture and moulds the character of her offspring.Keeping this in view, Ministry of Minority Affairs has reformulated the scheme in 2011-12 and renamed it as “Scheme for Leadership Development of Minority Women”. The implementation of the Scheme started in 2012-13.

Target Group and Distribution of Target
Target Group includes women belonging to all minorities notified under Section 2 (c) of the National Commission for Minorities Act, 1992 viz. Muslims, Sikhs, Christians, Buddhists and Zoroastrians (Parsis). the scheme permits a mix of women from non-minority communities not exceeding 25% of a project proposal.

We at AIM have undertaken to train 125 such women in areas around Begampur & Mongolpuri of Delhi which is a minority dominant area where as per a ground survey,women do want to take up education and understand the concept of spreading awareness in the family.

Objective :
The objective of the scheme is to empower and instill confidence among minority women, including their neighbours from other communities living in the same village/locality, by providing knowledge, tools and techniques for interacting with Government systems, banks and other institutions at all levels.

Leadership Development Training Modules
Specific training modules based on local issues/needs are being addressed as follows :

Learning Objectives -
1. To understand common childhood illness & immunization
2. Family planning & its methods
3. Government mechanism
4. Health & Hygiene
5. Home Management and how to adopt it
6. Issues related to women
7. Life skills in daily life & how to attain them for better life
8. Leadership of Women
9. Schemes/Programmes for welfare of Minorities
10. Public distribution system
11. To educate on the Government schemes available for minorities
12. Waste Water Sanitation and Solid Waste Disposal

CBSE

Need for Capacity building Programs in Schools
Quality concerns in school education are among the priorities of CBSE as they play a pivotal role in the development of the country. The Board is committed to make provisions for various training programmes for teachers and principals to enhance their understanding of curriculum and delivery mechanism and other professional qualities. T he training of the teachers assumes even greater significance in the wake of Right to Free and Compulsory Education Act-2009 to ensure the target achievement as per the proposed teacher student ratio proposed in the Act.


Outcomes Of The Capacity Building Programs:

Code: C.9: Leadership Training for Teachers:
At the end of the 6 hour session, the Teachers will be able to:
Understand the importance of Leadership as a Teacher
Analyze changes in behaviour and attitude when switching tasks and their relevance
Addressing the demands of all (Management, Students, Parents & Colleagues)
Choosing the right time to hop between the roles of a mentor, leader and a teacher

Code: D.3: Leadership & Innovation for Students:
We would be doing a 4 hour ‘Leadership and Innovation’ workshop for the students.
The students would be prepared to:
Realize their strengths.
Think and work like a leader.
Realize innovation is possible.
Learn to handle crisis and overcome obstacles.


Benefits to Schools:

Leadership Training for Teachers:
The workshop on ‘Leadership’ helps teachers understand their role as a leader who encourages students and becomes their role model. A teacher has to play several roles besides being a teacher. She has to co-ordinate with students, management, colleagues and parents. So the workshop helps the teachers to do multi-tasking, manageself while managing students, management and other external stake holders along with managing time and set priorities. As a result it helps the teachers to meet different calls with enthusiasm and without tension.

Leadership & Innovation Training for Students:
The workshop on ‘Leadership and Innovation’ helps students identify their potential to lead and become creative. According to Denis Waitley ,
“You have all the reason in the world to achieve your grandest dreams. Imagination plus innovation equals realization. ”
The workshop helps students realize that achieving dreams is possible. Though, mere dreaming is not enough. Rather the inner urge to do things differently and help others, must exist. This workshop provides impetus to students to start thinking and working differently.
