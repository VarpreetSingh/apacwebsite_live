ATHENAEUM LEARNING RESOURCE CENTER






Books
The book amassment of library comprises books and reference resources. They are housed in stacks in the Ground Floor (Text Books, Fiction and Literature Books), First Floor (Reference Books) and are arranged as per Dewey Decimal Relegation. Each book is assigned a number representing the subject and arranged in ascending order in open shelves. Some accumulation (Training Manuals and Scholastic Videos and AIM Events Photographs Album) is in closed shelves and please contact circulation desk or library to refer them. To browse and search for this amassment use online catalogue

Books Arrangement

Books in the library are arranged in Dewey Decimal Classification Scheme. After locating an item in the OPAC, the screen will provide the call number for the item. The call number is the “address” of the item on the shelf.

It will look something like the following:
658 (assigned to the book “Management”)
658.8 (assigned to the book for “Marketing”)
330 (assigned to the book for “Economics”)
