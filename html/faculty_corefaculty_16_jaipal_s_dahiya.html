Faculty Profile


INDIVISUAL PROFILE

Dr.Jaipal S. Dahiya

Professor

Area : IB & Economics and Strategic Management

Experience:

Ph.D.

Contact Details

jaipal.dahiya@asiapacific.edu
+91-1234567890



Dr. Dahiya holds Master’s degree in Economics from Kurukshetra University and Ph.D. in Economics from Jamia Millia Islamia, New Delhi. He has an impressive teaching and research profile. Before joining Asia-Pacific Institute of Management he has taught at J.K Business School, Gurgaon and ERA Business School, Dwarka for about five years. Prior to that Dr. Dahiya hastaught at MDIGurgaon for about thirty years.He hasbeen visitingscholar to J.L. Kellogg Graduate School of Management, Northwestern University, U.S.A. under UNDP Scholar Exchange Programme. His areas of teaching interest are; 1) Economic Environment and Policy (Macroeconomics). This course he has taught to PGP students as also to the participants of National Management Programme (NMP) and Executive Management Programme (part time) course at MDI. Also he taught this course at NOIDA Campus of IIM Lucknow to the participants of Armed Forces programme. He taught this course at EBS and JKBS Gurgaon. 2) Managerial Economics(Microeconomics). Prof. Dahiya taught this course to the students of PGP at ERA Business School, ITMU andat JKBS Gurgaon. 3) International Economics- This course he has taught to PGP students as elective, to NMP participants and to the Executive Management Programme students at MDI. 4) Business and its Environment-.Dr. Dahiya has taught this course to PGP students at JBKPS Gurgaon and EBS Dwarka. . 5) Strategic Management- Dr.Dahiya has taught this course to JK Business School students. In research and consultancy, his interest areas are in social development schemes focusing improvement in the Quality of Human Capital, Local Area Development and Rural Development. He has conducted about a dozen research studies sponsored by multinational agencies, financial institutions and area development agencies. He has also conducted about two dozen consultancies mostly sponsored by National Literacy Mission of MHRD, GOI. They were focused on developing new schemes, restructuring the existing schemes and on impact evaluation of existing schemes. DAE- MHRD, GOI, has published some of his reports. He was member of UNESCO- NLM search committeefor three years. The objective of the committee was to identify the best performing NGO or government sponsored autonomous institutions/agencies for UNESCO-NLM award. Dr. Dahiya has organized about a dozen MDPs for the executives of private and public sector undertakings, financial institutions and banks. These programmes were focused on project appraisal and financing. He has also organized about six MDPs for Directors of State Resource Centers, an agency sponsored and funded by NLM, MHRD government of India. The programmes were focused on general management. These agencies are engaged in implementation of social development schemes. After retirement from MDI at the age of 62, he has worked as professor of Economics at ERA Business School Dwarka and JK Business School Bhondasi, Gurgaon and presently working in Asia-Pacific Institute of Management Delhi.
