INFORMATION!



Asia-Pacific Institute of Management offers admissions to its two year full time PGDM program and other sectoral programs namely, Post Graduate Diploma in Management-General (PGDM), Post Graduate Diploma in Management-Marketing (PGDM - MKT), Post Graduate Diploma in Management-International Business (PGDM- IB), Post Graduate Diploma in Management-Banking and Financial Services (PGDM- BFS) and Three Years Part Time Executive Post Graduate Diploma in Management (EX-PGDM) at its New Delhi campus for the 2018-20 batch.

Interested Students can find details of the selection criteria, how to apply, the fee structure and financial assistance available also download the Information Bulletin 2018 and apply online for admissions for the 22nd Academic Session.
