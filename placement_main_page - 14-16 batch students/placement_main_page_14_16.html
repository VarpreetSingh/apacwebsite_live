Centre for Corporate Relations (CCR) is the hub of activities which drive the 3-I (Institute - Industry - Interface) initiative. CCR develops and maintains a close relationship with corporate through guest lectures, corporate get-togethers, industry visits and frequent interaction at both institute and student level.

The CCR arranges for the mandatory six-to eight weeks Summer Internship Program (SIP) in corporate houses for each student at the end of the first year. The CCR is also responsible for organizing final placement for the graduating students - a continuous process that effectively starts the day a student joins the institute.

The Institute saw very vibrant placements for the 2015-17 batch with over 120 companies coming for recruiting our students. Some of the prominent recruiters include EY, Copal Amba, Moodyâ€™s Analytics, Continental Tyres, ICICI Bank, Kotak Mahindra Bank, Decathlon, FedEx, Lloyd Ventures, Zycus Infotech. All the 2016-18 batch students got SIP with stipend and very good projects to work on with MNCs, Banks, FMCG and other good companies.

Students are actively involved in the activities of CCR - which includes publication of placement brochure and other literature, interaction with recruiters, organising HR Roundtable, working out placement strategies & scheduling activities, maintaining resume banks, organizing industrial visits, speakers of eminence from industry etc. The exposure to this complex process helps to develop their Participative Managerial Effectiveness (PME).
